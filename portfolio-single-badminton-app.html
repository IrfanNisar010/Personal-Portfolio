Badminton App

A sleek and intuitive mobile application design for booking badminton courts efficiently. The prototype focuses on simplifying the user journey from finding a venue to confirming a slot, ensuring a seamless experience for sports enthusiasts.

Mobile App UI/UX
Services UI/UX Designer
Category Mobile App Design
Client Personal Project

Challenge.

Booking a badminton court often involves navigating disjointed menus, dealing with schedule conflicts, and unclear pricing setups. The challenge was to design a comprehensive booking solution addressing these pain points, creating a frictionless user interface that athletes could use on the go without frustration.

Solution.

I structured a clean, modern interface focusing on a visualized court selection system and clear pricing breakdowns. A dedicated user profile section was added to manage past and upcoming games seamlessly. The visual language leveraged vibrant, sport-centric colors dynamically contrasted against dark elements to maintain excellent readability and accessibility standards.

Results.

The final prototype delivered a streamlined UX flow that theoretically reduces booking times and eliminates calendar conflicts for court managers.

  • Visual representation of active slots
  • Intuitive onboarding and profile management
  • Accessible high-contrast UI tailored for active users
