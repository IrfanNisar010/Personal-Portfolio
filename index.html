How do I start working with you?

Super simple just reach out through my contact form or DM me. We’ll hop on a quick call, figure out what you’re trying to build, and I’ll guide you through the best direction. If everything feels right, we move forward with timelines and deliverables.

What design tools do you use?

For design, I primarily work with Figma.
For development, I use the MERN stack, various modern UI libraries, and whatever tech fits the project best.
My workflow is clean and optimized — no messy files, no confusing layers.

How long does a project take?

It depends on the size of the project, but here’s a general idea:

Small UI work: 2–4 days
Medium website design: 1–2 weeks
Full-stack projects: 3–6 weeks
I always give a clear timeline before starting, so you’ll never be left guessing.

Do you provide revisions?

Yes. I want the final output to feel perfect to you.
Each project comes with revision rounds to polish the visuals, improve flow, and tune micro-interactions until it hits the vibe you want.

What industries do you work with?

I’m industry-agnostic — if your project needs clean design and thoughtful experiences, I’m in.
So far, I’ve worked on tech platforms, e-commerce, personal brands, and experimental UI projects.

Do you offer development services?

Yes.
I don’t just design — I also build.
If you need a live, responsive, fully functional website, I can take your design all the way to deployment using Node.js, MongoDB, Express, and modern front-end tools.

What is your pricing structure?

My pricing is flexible and based on the project scope.
Once I understand what you need — design, development, or both — I’ll give you a clear quote with no hidden add-ons.

Can you redesign my existing website?

Definitely.
If your current site feels outdated, slow, or just doesn’t match the level you want, I can redesign it from the ground up — modern UI, cleaner UX, better structure, and performance that actually feels fast.
